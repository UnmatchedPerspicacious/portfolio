My Entrepreneurial Journey

My Entrepreneurial Journey

My journey began at the age of 13, when I asked my dad a simple question: “How does the stock market work?” That moment sparked a deep interest in trading and investing. I quickly transitioned from curiosity to action — studying how markets operate, making small investments, and eventually exploring high-frequency trading (HFT).

As my passion grew, I taught myself how to code, enabling me to build tools that interact directly with live financial markets. I developed backtesting engines, data pipelines, and trading bots — all part of understanding how to combine technical knowledge with market dynamics.

One of the most ambitious projects I’ve built is Tradewave — a platform that allows users to connect their brokerage accounts via API and start automated trading using a built-in algorithm. The goal was to simplify algorithmic trading and make it accessible in just a few setup steps.

I've invested a tremendous amount of time and effort into Tradewave. While it's currently publicly accessible, it's still under heavy development. The algorithms need to be improved and refined to provide consistent value and profitability. Despite the technical progress, I’ve come to recognize the legal and regulatory barriers involved in operating such a platform. Tradewave remains a long-term vision that I plan to pursue rigorously after I turn 18, when I can navigate those requirements more effectively.

In parallel with my interest in HFT, I also studied the principles of long-term investing — particularly those championed by Warren Buffett. This led me to explore fundamental analysis and financial statement evaluation. By applying these principles, I was able to successfully identify undervalued opportunities like NMIH (National Mortgage Insurance Holdings), which stood out due to its low P/E ratio and above-average profit margins for its industry.

At present, I’m focusing on two more practical and scalable ventures:

CryptoVision – A crypto analytics platform powered by AI, designed to provide actionable market insights, buy/sell signals, and trend analysis. I’m actively developing the platform, and plan to officially launch and monetize it after I turn 18.

Fitness Influencer Partnership – I’m helping a friend monetize his fitness program by connecting him with fitness influencers. He supplies the product, the influencers market it in exchange for a commission, and I earn by brokering the relationships — creating a sustainable, win-win-win model for all parties involved.

These projects allow me to generate momentum, gain business experience, and develop revenue streams while continuing to build the foundation for larger-scale ventures like Tradewave.

Currently, I’m in a preparation phase — developing multiple projects, studying the strategies of successful entrepreneurs, and sharpening the technical and business skills I’ll need to build profitable, scalable ventures. Every product I work on is created with long-term value, monetization, and growth in mind.

Once I turn 18, I plan to fully deploy CryptoVision, return to Tradewave with renewed focus, and begin executing at a higher level — combining everything I’ve learned into ventures that deliver real value.
